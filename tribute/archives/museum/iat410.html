Why it matters

The preserved deck lists XenoHammer - IGF Student Showcase 2002 among prior showcase winners. That gives us a later academic citation showing the project still had enough visibility to be used as an example in a university course.

It is also a nice bridge between the original 2001-2002 release window and the broader student-game lineage that followed.
